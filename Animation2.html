CSS Animation Properties

1. animation-name

Uses the animation named moveBox.

2. animation-duration

The animation takes 5 seconds to complete.

3. animation-delay

The animation starts after 2 seconds.

4. animation-iteration-count

The animation runs 3 times.

5. animation-timing-function

ease-in-out starts slowly, becomes fast, then slows again.

6. animation-direction

alternate moves forward, then backward.

7. animation-fill-mode

forwards keeps the final position after the animation ends.
